Who Is Dawson Mudenyo?

Faith, Discipline & Service

Born and raised in Lunyu as the son of a Bishop, Dawson Mudenyo grew up shaped by faith, humility, discipline, and a deep heart for service. From Liyavo Primary School to Kitale Day High School, the values instilled by his family and community became the unshakeable foundation of who he is today.

His worldview expanded dramatically at a missionary school in Chicago, Illinois, where he immersed himself in global humanitarian work. That experience opened his eyes to what disciplined, accountable governance can achieve — and planted the conviction that Kwanza deserves the same standard of leadership.

Today, as an international NGO expert, Dawson advises organisations around the world on governance, strategic planning, and capacity development. He is bringing two decades of that hard-won expertise home — to fight for Kwanza Constituency as its Member of Parliament in the 2027 General Election.
