Who Is Dawson Mudenyo?

A Son of Lunyu, Shaped by Faith

Born and raised in Lunyu as the son of a Bishop, Dawson Mudenyo grew up shaped by faith, humility, discipline, and a deep heart for service. From Liyavo Primary School to Kitale Day High School, the values of his community became the foundation of who he is today.

"My worldview expanded at a missionary school in Chicago, Illinois — but everything I learned out there, I am bringing back home, to Kwanza."

Today, as an international NGO expert, Dawson advises organisations on governance, strategic planning, and capacity development. He is running for Member of Parliament for Kwanza in 2027 to bring that same discipline, transparency, and global best practice directly to the people who raised him.
