Dawson Mudenyo Officially Launches MP Bid for Kwanza Constituency

Hon. Dawson Mudenyo has formally announced his candidacy for Member of Parliament, Kwanza Constituency, in the 2027 General Election. Speaking to residents in Kwanza Town, he outlined a vision grounded in faith, transparency, and two decades of international NGO governance experience — pledging to bring the same rigor to healthcare, education, water, and infrastructure across all four wards.
